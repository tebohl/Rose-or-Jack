A Night to Remember?

On the evening of December 17, 1997, the film Titanic was released, directed, written, produced, and co-edited by James Cameron. To some, it was an unprecedented, cinematic achievement. Others were left wondering, "Didn't the Titanic sink in less time than this film is taking...?"


This machine learning model uses actual Titanic passenger records and survival data to predict if you will make it through James Cameron's epic based on your age, sex, chosen passenger class, and more.
